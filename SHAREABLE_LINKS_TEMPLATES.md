## 🎯 Best Practices for Sharing

### ✅ DO:
- Share the actual link (provides real value)
- Include the score (compelling metric)
- Mention the tier (easy to understand)
- Use clear language (avoid jargon)
- Include call-to-action (encourage viewing)

### ❌ DON'T:
- Over-exaggerate results
- Cherry-pick only positive dimensions
- Share incomplete information
- Use misleading headlines
- Claim certifications you don't have

## 🎓 How to Use These Templates

1. **Select a template** that matches your audience
2. **Replace placeholders**:
   - `[MODEL_NAME]` → Your model name
   - `[SCORE]` → Your actual score
   - `[LINK]` → Your shareable link
   - etc.
3. **Customize** with your specific details
4. **Copy & paste** to your platform
5. **Share!** ✨

---

## 📊 Template Effectiveness Tips

**Most Effective Elements**:
1. Specific score (e.g., "765/900" vs "high")
2. Visual elements (emoji, formatting)
3. Clear benefits (what this means)
4. Call-to-action (link to view full results)
5. Credibility markers (certification ID, verification)

**Engagement Tips**:
- Ask a question to encourage comments
- Tag relevant people/organizations
- Use relevant hashtags
- Post at optimal times for your platform
- Include visual (generate QR code if available)
